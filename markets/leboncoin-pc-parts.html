French marketplace guide

How to find PC parts on Leboncoin

Leboncoin is an important French marketplace for local second-hand goods. Its hardware inventory includes individual components, bundles and complete desktop PCs, but listings vary greatly in technical detail.

Search in French and by model

Use the product model wherever possible, then add French category terms. “Carte graphique” finds graphics cards, “processeur” finds CPUs, “carte mère” finds motherboards and “mémoire RAM” finds memory. “PC gamer” can find complete systems, but those results need careful specification checks.

PCPartDeals helps organize supported external listings, making it easier to browse hardware categories. The source listing on Leboncoin remains the place to confirm availability, location and transaction details.

Clarify vague descriptions

A seller may know the computer works without knowing the exact model of every part. Ask for screenshots from system information tools or clear photographs of labels. An “i7 gaming PC” could contain several generations with very different performance. A graphics card listing should state the full model and memory capacity.

Bundles require separate valuation. A CPU, board and RAM combination may be convenient, but check BIOS support, memory generation and whether the original accessories are included. Do not pay extra for components that are obsolete or unusable in your planned build.

Safety and transaction checks

Review the seller profile and listing history. Ask for current photos when the images look like manufacturer material. If shipping is involved, confirm how the item will be packed and use the platform's current protected process where available. Avoid urgent requests to move payment or communication away from the marketplace.

For local collection, agree on whether testing is possible. A complete PC can be shown booting and running a workload. Individual GPUs or motherboards may be harder to test, so the condition evidence and price should reflect that limitation.

Compare beyond France

Leboncoin may have strong local inventory, but it is still useful to compare equivalent offers from other European sources. Shipping, language and payment rules may make a foreign listing less practical, yet wider comparison helps establish a realistic market price.

Frequently asked questions

Which French terms help find PC parts?

Try carte graphique, processeur, carte mère, mémoire RAM, SSD, disque dur and pièces PC alongside the exact model.

Does PCPartDeals handle Leboncoin payments?

No. PCPartDeals helps users discover listings; transactions remain with the original marketplace and seller.
