Where to buy PC parts? 3 best places in Europe

There are multiple options where yo start buying computer parts for your next PC build. Here are my favourites!

Published: May 2026 5 min read

Best places in Europe

The best place to buy PC parts safely has to be Vinted. Vinted mainly focuses on second-hand clothing, but it has thousands of listings of GPUs, CPUs, RAM and SSDs. It has better pricing and safe payments.

My second favourite is Yaga.ee (Only operates in Estonia and Latvia) because you can find some amazing deals there due to sellers being regular parents who just sell their kid's old equipment for cheap. I personally prefer Vinted because there are more options to choose from.

If you live in Estonia, then you may have heard about Osta. Osta.ee is an Estonian version of Ebay. There are auctions and buy-now sales there. PC parts are more expensive there compared to Vinted.

If you live in Europe

You should check out these marketplaces when picking out the PC parts for your upcoming PC build.

  • Vinted
  • Yaga
  • Osta
