Searching for components in Spain

Wallapop carries a wide range of private listings in Spain, from low-cost office hardware to modern gaming parts. Combine the exact model with terms such as “tarjeta gráfica,” “procesador,” “placa base,” “memoria RAM,” “disco duro,” “SSD” and “componentes PC.” Search nearby cities when local pickup is practical.

PCPartDeals can surface supported external listings in a consistent category feed. Always open the source offer to confirm the seller, location, current price and delivery options.

Evaluate complete PCs and bundles

Complete gaming computers are convenient but easy to describe vaguely. Ask for the exact CPU, GPU, motherboard, power supply, RAM arrangement and storage models. A listing that mentions only “i7” or “NVIDIA graphics” does not provide enough information to judge performance or value.

For bundles, check whether every component has been tested together. A motherboard and CPU may require a BIOS update, and an included cooler may not have the correct mounting hardware. Price each useful component separately before deciding whether the package is genuinely cheaper.

Condition and payment

Ask for current photos and a clear description of defects. For a graphics card, request a recent benchmark or gaming test. For SSDs and HDDs, request health data. For RAM, ask whether a memory test has completed without errors. A seller who cannot provide technical evidence may still be legitimate, but the price should reflect the uncertainty.

Use the platform's current protected payment and shipping options where appropriate. Avoid moving immediately to a private transfer or messaging channel. If collecting locally, choose a safe meeting arrangement and test valuable hardware when possible.

Compare the real cost

Include shipping, service charges and replacement accessories. Also compare against current Spanish retail pricing, because new budget components sometimes offer better efficiency and a warranty for only a little more. Used hardware is most compelling when the performance difference is meaningful and the condition evidence is strong.
